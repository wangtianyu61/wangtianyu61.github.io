Summary of Research Experiences

I am deeply impressed by the theoretical and practical beauty of operations research at the end of the second year of my undergraduate studies. During my exchange life at National University of Singapore (NUS), I was fortunate to be advised by Professor Melvyn Sim and co-advised by Professor Chun Wang in robust portfolio optimization. I gradually found the great charm in the interplay between machine learning, statistics and optimization.

Motivated by the relationship between optimization and statistics in this era, I went on designing better procedures in estimation and optimization steps in data-driven revenue management working with Professor Chenye Wu in a SRT project.

During 2020 Summer, I am delighted to work on the project related to model selection in contextual bandit and supervised online by Professor David Simchi-Levi.
